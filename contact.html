Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our driving school

How do I book my first driving lesson?

You can book your first lesson by filling out the contact form, calling us directly, or messaging us on WhatsApp. We'll get back to you within 24 hours to schedule your first lesson.

What should I bring to my first lesson?

For your first lesson, please bring your G1 license, comfortable shoes for driving, and any glasses or contacts if you require them for driving.

Do you offer pick-up and drop-off services?

Yes, we offer pick-up and drop-off services within our service areas for all driving lessons and road tests.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Mississauga, Brampton, Oakville, and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm if we serve your specific location.
