Services

Comprehensive Driving Services

From beginner to advanced, we offer a complete range of driving instruction services to meet all your needs

Beginner Driver Education

Complete G1 to G2 license program including 20 hours of classroom theory, 10 hours of in-car training, and MTO certification.

  • Traffic rules & regulations
  • Vehicle control basics
  • Road safety fundamentals

G2 Road Test Preparation

Specialized training to prepare for your G2 road test with practice on test routes and common test scenarios.

  • Test route familiarization
  • Parallel parking practice
  • Emergency stop procedures

G License Training

Advanced driving instruction for G license including highway driving, complex intersections, and defensive techniques.

  • Highway driving skills
  • Advanced intersection techniques
  • Defensive driving strategies

Individual Lessons

Customized one-on-one instruction tailored to your specific needs and learning pace.

  • Personalized instruction
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Focus on weak areas

Road Test Services

Complete road test support including vehicle provision, test booking, and pre-test warm-up lessons.

  • Instructor's car for test
  • Test booking assistance
  • Pre-test practice session

Refresher Courses

For experienced drivers who want to improve skills, regain confidence, or learn new techniques.

  • Skill assessment
  • Confidence building
  • Modern driving techniques
