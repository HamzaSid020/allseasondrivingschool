Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our driving programs and services

We offer comprehensive driving instruction including:

  • Beginner Driver Education (BDE) programs
  • G2 and G license training
  • Individual driving lessons
  • Road test preparation
  • Car rental for driving tests
  • Refresher courses for experienced drivers
  • Defensive driving techniques

We offer competitive pricing with several package options:

  • Package #1: Complete BDE Program - $600 (HST & Certification Fees included)
  • Package #2: BDE + Road Test - $820 (HST & Certification Fees included)
  • Package #3: Premium BDE Program - $1000 (HST & Certification Fees included)
  • Individual Lessons: Starting at $50 per hour for G2, $55 per hour for G license

Yes, we offer free pick-up and drop-off service within our service areas including Mississauga, Brampton, Oakville, Etobicoke, and surrounding areas. This service is included with all our driving packages and individual lessons.

We have a fair cancellation policy to accommodate our students:

  • 24-hour notice required for lesson cancellations
  • Late cancellations (less than 24 hours) may result in 50% charge
  • No-shows will be charged the full lesson fee
  • Emergency situations are considered on a case-by-case basis

Yes, we provide well-maintained, fully insured vehicles for road tests. Our cars meet all Ministry of Transportation requirements and include dual controls for safety. This service is included in our Package #2 and Package #3, and available as an add-on for individual lessons.

We pride ourselves on our exceptional success rate:

  • 98% first-time pass rate for G2 road tests
  • 95% first-time pass rate for G license tests
  • 99% overall success rate within two attempts
  • Consistently high student satisfaction ratings

Yes, all our instructors are:

  • Fully certified by the Ministry of Transportation Ontario (MTO)
  • Experienced professionals with years of teaching experience
  • Trained in defensive driving techniques
  • Patient and professional with students of all skill levels
  • First-aid and CPR certified

Booking lessons is easy and convenient:

  • Phone: Call us at (647) 770-7811
  • Email: allseasonds@gmail.com
  • Website: Fill out our contact form
  • WhatsApp: Message us directly
  • In-person: Visit our location in Mississauga

We offer flexible scheduling including weekends and evenings.

We accept various payment methods for your convenience:

  • Cash
  • Debit cards
  • Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard)
  • E-transfer
  • Payment plans available for package deals

Payment is typically due before the start of lessons or as arranged for package deals.

Yes, we understand that our students have busy schedules, so we offer flexible lesson times:

  • Weekend lessons (Saturday and Sunday)
  • Evening lessons (until 9:00 PM)
  • Early morning sessions
  • Holiday availability
  • Last-minute booking options when available
